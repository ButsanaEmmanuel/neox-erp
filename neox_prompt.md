A premium, futuristic ERP dashboard for Neox, featuring a glassmorphic dark theme with vibrant data visualizations and smooth micro-animations.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark Mode, Glassmorphism, Premium "Antigravity" style
- Background: Deep Obsidian Black (#0a0a0c) with subtle radial gradients of Cosmic Purple (#1a0033) and Deep Navy (#000d1a).
- Surfaces: Frosted glass panels (#ffffff08) with a 1px soft white border (#ffffff14) and 12px backdrop-blur.
- Primary Accent: Electric Cyan (#00f2ff) for high-priority actions and active navigation.
- Secondary Accent: Neon Magenta (#ff00ff) for financial indicators and "growth" metrics.
- Positive Action: Emerald Green (#10b981) for safety (HSE) success.
- Warning/Alert: Solar Orange (#f97316) for SCM bottlenecks.
- Typography: "Outfit" or "Inter" (Sans-serif), bold headings, monospaced data values for a technical feel.
- Effects: Soft outer glows on primary buttons, floating/levitating card elevations, micro-animations on hover.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Glass Sidebar:** Slim, elegant navigation bar on the left with glowing icons for:
   - Dashboard (Home)
   - Finance Management
   - SCM & Sales
   - HRM (Human Resources)
   - HSE (Health, Safety, Environment)
   - Global Search & Settings
2. **Global Header:** "NEOX COMMAND CENTER" title in a futuristic typeface, user profile widget with glassmorphism, and a global "Antigravity" toggle switch (visual only).
3. **Hero Analytics Grid:**
   - **Finance Overview:** A large, glowing area chart showing revenue trends.
   - **SCM Pulse:** A vertical scrollable list of active shipments with pulsing status pips.
   - **HRM Glance:** A "People" widget showing current headcount and employee happiness index.
   - **HSE Shield:** A circular gauge for safety compliance, glowing green for 100%.
4. **Quick Actions Float:** A floating action button group in the bottom-right for "New Transaction", "Log HSE Incident", and "Quick Report".
5. **Footer:** "Powered by Neox" in low-opacity text with a live system status indicator.
