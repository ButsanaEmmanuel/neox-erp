# Design System: Neox ERP
**Project ID:** 2641824781524433786

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Neox ERP is built on the "Antigravity" aesthetic—a premium, futuristic design philosophy that emphasizes depth, clarity, and levitation. The interface uses glassmorphism to create a sense of multi-layered intelligence, where data feels suspended in a cosmic or ethereal space. 

- **Dark Mode:** Deep, Obsidian-based space with cosmic purple and navy gradients.
- **Light Mode:** Airy, Cloud-based ethereal space with soft sky-blue and pearl gradients.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Dark Mode (Core)
- **Obsidian Space (#0a0a0c):** Primary page background.
- **Electric Cyan (#00f2ff):** Primary accent for navigation, active states, and high-priority data.
- **Neon Magenta (#ff00ff):** Secondary accent for financial growth and pulse indicators.
- **Emerald Safety (#10b981):** Positive feedback and HSE compliance.
- **Solar Warning (#f97316):** Alerts and SCM bottlenecks.

### Light Mode (Ethereal)
- **Pearl Ethereal (#f8fafc):** Primary page background with soft #e2e8f0 gradients.
- **Deep Velocity Blue (#0284c7):** High-contrast primary accent for readability.
- **Vibrant Amethyst (#9333ea):** Secondary accent for financial metrics.
- **Natural Moss (#16a34a):** HSE success indicators.
- **Sunset Ember (#ea580c):** Critical alerts.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Family:** "Outfit" or "Inter" for a modern, clean look. "Space Grotesk" for technical headers.
- **Data Values:** Monospaced fonts (e.g., "JetBrains Mono" or "Roboto Mono") to emphasize precision and technicality.
- **Hierarchy:** Bold, oversized headings for primary metrics; medium weight for navigation labels.

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** 
    - **Physicality:** Subtly rounded (8px) or pill-shaped (full) for high-impact actions.
    - **Effects:** Soft outer glows matching the accent color.
- **Cards/Containers:** 
    - **Physicality:** Generously rounded (16px - 24px) frosted glass panels.
    - **Transparency:** 5% - 10% opacity with a high backdrop-blur (12px - 20px).
    - **Border:** 1px "Silk Border"—ultra-thin, low-opacity white (#ffffff14) or gray (#00000008).
- **Inputs/Forms:** 
    - **Style:** Inset glass backgrounds with subtle white/black strokes. Transparent on focus with a glowing border.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Margins:** Generous whitespace to allow components to "breathe" and feel lighter.
- **Floating HUD:** Use fixed or absolute positioning for high-priority navigation bars and quick action buttons to enhance the levitating feel.
- **Grids:** Strict 8px grid system for spacing, but with "soft" alignment where shadows and blurs create organic transitions.
